# Part 3 Showcase

This is a quick Part 3 show-and-tell, not a formal viva.

Date: Tuesday 9 June 2026, 11am-1pm, LT6.

There are `9` groups. Each group gets `5` minutes to present and `2` minutes
for questions. It is worth `10` marks, so keep it focused and do not overbuild
it.

The mood should be technical, casual, and fun: show us what you made, what you
tried, and one or two things another group might find useful.

## The Vibe

Start with the animation. Then tell us the quick story behind it.

You might show a scene editor screenshot, a before/after comparison, a prompt
that worked, a camera trick, a motion clip that saved the day, or an artifact
that made everyone laugh. Tips and tricks are very welcome.

No need to walk through every command or make a polished pitch deck. A rough
but honest "we tried this, it broke, this tiny fix helped" is exactly the kind
of thing worth sharing.

## Q&A

The questions will be short and friendly. We may ask how you made something,
what tool or trick helped most, what artifact remains, or what you would try
next.

Brief answers are fine. The goal is to share what you learned.
